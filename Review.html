The Guideline Change Management Process

Review

Step 1

The Guideline Oversight Committee will meet annually to review all proposed guideline updates for endorsement. A Final Draft must be presented to the Committee prior to the advertised meeting dates. The review material will include the Final Draft, Stakeholder Feedback Register, and the Guideline Change Proposal.

Step 2

Feedback from the Committee will be distributed to the relevant Guideline Owners. There will be an opportunity to respond to feedback and make changes necessary for endorsement.
