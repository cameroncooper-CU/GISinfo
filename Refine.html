The Guideline Change Management Process

Refine

Step 1

After Guideline Oversight Committee endorsement has been given, the proposed Guideline is reviewed by a specialist Technical Editor to ensure quality and consistency across all Guidelines.

The Technical Editing process is started by Drawing Services. The Guideline Owner will be consulted through the entire process.

Step 2

After the Technical Editing process is completed, the revised Guideline is given a release number and is marked “Approved for Release.”
