Steps to managing your Guidelines:

  1. Download and complete the Guideline Change Request Form.
  2. Send the completed form to drawingservices@curtin.edu.au to start the endorsement process.
  3. Once the change has been endorsed, you will receive links to an editable copy of the current guideline together with a stakeholder feedback register, if applicable.
  4. Complete your updates to the guideline and the stakeholder feedback register on SharePoint.
  5. Notify drawingservices@curtin.edu.au to start the final approval process, if required.
  6. You will be advised when your guideline has gone live on the PF&D Properties website.
