The Guideline Change Management Process

Consult

Step 1

The stakeholder consultation period begins once the majority of the updates have been made to the guideline. Your guideline will be shared with your nominated stakeholders via Sharepoint. Stakeholders can add comments and suggestions directly to the document.

Depending on the complexity and scale of the changes and the number of stakeholders, the consultation period may be as short as one week or as long as several months.

Step 2

After the consultation period has completed. A Stakeholder Feedback Register is completed to capture all relevant comments and feedback. This register is attached to the version Change Log History.
